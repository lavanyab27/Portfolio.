A Regional Spirit

Jaisalmer Gin is rooted in Rajasthan's desert botanicals and artisan traditions. The challenge was to position it as both premium and authentically local ‚Äî appealing to discerning consumers without sacrificing regional identity.

02 ¬∑ Research

Market & Context

Challenges

Differentiation

In a crowded premium gin market, many brands lean on European heritage or minimalist Nordic aesthetics. Jaisalmer needed to own its regional story without feeling inauthentic.

Craft vs. Scale

Early-stage small-batch production required packaging that could scale cost-effectively while retaining tactile, artisanal cues that justify premium pricing.

Sustainability Expectations

Modern spirits consumers prioritise minimal packaging, recyclable materials, and transparent production narratives.

Strategy & Concept

01

Desert Provenance

Centre the narrative around Jaisalmer's landscape, botanicals and craft. Use visual cues (sandstone textures, block-print patterns) that signal place.

02

Tactile Luxury

Prioritise tactile finishes: subtle embossing, soft-touch varnish on labels, and a warm matte paper to communicate premium feel without heavy packaging.

03

Botanical Minimalism

Rather than dense illustration, use modular botanical elements and negative space to create an elegant, calm label composition.

04

Cultural Collaboration

Collaborate with local artisans to feature authentic craft techniques and provide a provenance narrative that supports the brand's sustainability story.
