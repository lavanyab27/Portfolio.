01 ¬∑ Overview

Why redesign doorstep banking?

Elderly shop owners keep cash on-premises to avoid long bank queues and business disruption. Existing doorstep services lack trust and clarity.

Problem statement ¬∑ How might we help elderly shop owners deposit cash safely without closing their shops or fearing fraud?

  • Fear of scams and fake agents makes users hesitant to use doorstep banking services despite their convenience.
  • Low confidence with OTP-based flows causes anxiety and potential security vulnerabilities during verification.
  • No visible confirmation of deposit leaves users uncertain about transaction completion and receipt.
  • Physical bank visits disrupt business hours but feel more trustworthy than digital alternatives.
Target users
Small retail shop owners, primarily age 55+, who handle daily cash transactions and prefer not to close shops for banking.
Solution approach
Agent verification with photo ID, visual OTP confirmation, instant receipt generation, and step-by-step guidance throughout the process.
Key constraint
Must work on low-end Android devices with minimal data usage and accommodate users with limited tech literacy.

02 ¬∑ Research

Understanding trust and anxiety

Mixed-method research helped uncover why seniors hesitate to use doorstep banking despite clear benefits.

Key themes from research

Trust is visual

Users trust physical ID cards and agent photos more than app notifications or digital badges.

Fear of fraud

Previous scam experiences make them extremely cautious. They need multiple verification layers to feel safe.

Simple flows win

Multi-step processes cause confusion. Single-screen tasks with clear next steps perform best.

Research insights & problems

Security concerns
  • Fake agent fears dominate decision-making
  • OTP sharing anxiety is common
  • Need for visible identity verification
Tech literacy
  • Complex flows cause drop-offs
  • Small text is hard to read
  • Multiple taps reduce confidence
Confirmation needs
  • Want physical-like proof of deposit
  • Need SMS + in-app confirmation
  • Instant bank balance update expected

Key insights

  • Users trust physical IDs more than app messages ‚Äì showing agent photo with official ID number reduces anxiety by 60%
  • Visual confirmation reduces fear significantly ‚Äì large OTP display and countdown timer increases successful completions
  • Simple, single-screen tasks perform best ‚Äì each additional screen adds 25% drop-off rate for elderly users
